ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

What is this website?

This Website is a platform wherein you are able to learn a few things about numerical systems whilst learning to calculate the numerical systems itselfthrough the use of this website's very own Numerical Conversion Calculator, wherein you will calculate the given integers you have typed to itself Decimal, Binary, Octal and Hexadecimal form. And with the help of the "Number System Conversion Table", you will be given a format as to how exactly the numbers work whilst also learning more about the "Numerical Sytems" itself. This platforms helps you expand a much broader but a simpler understanding of the concept of the Numerical Systems in our world as these are specifically very convenient for upcoming learners and students who insist in pursuingthe technological aspect of today's modern technologies that make use of such Numerical Systems.
